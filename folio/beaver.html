Mechatronic Beaver and Game

I worked on a team of five to design and build a mechatronic beaver for Design Nature, a first-year design course. We were given six weeks and a $50 budget to construct an animal-themed game experience for fourth graders.

Benji the beaver is comprised of a delrin chassis and two tank driven rear legs. The electronics are waterproofed, giving the beaver the capability to swim. Out of the sixteen teams in the course, we were one of the three teams that attempted a water animal.
